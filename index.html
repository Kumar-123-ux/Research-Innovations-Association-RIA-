About RIA

RIA is an Indian foundation focused on promoting rigorous scientific research and practical innovations. Our goal is to support researchers and innovators in making impactful contributions to society.

Vision: To foster a culture of research excellence and innovative problem-solving in India.

Values: Scientific Integrity · Curiosity · Collaboration · Societal Impact
